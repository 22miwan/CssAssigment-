Summary

The Technical Institute of DarbandiKhan is a branch of the Ministry of Higher Education established in 29/09/2005 under the Council of Ministersâ€™ official order (Issue No. 2312). It initially consisted of two departments (Electricity and Accountancy). The Institute was established in order to provide the Darbandi Khan high school students the opportunity to apply for a local institute and to gain technical diploma in a variety of areas offering them the opportunity to gain jobs in the Governmental and private sectors.
